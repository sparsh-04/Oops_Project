Q. I have forgotten my password. How do I reset it?

Go to the Login page and select the "Forgot Password" option. Then type the email for which you created the account. Click enter. An email will be sent to you containing the link to change your password. Change the password. Try not to forget it this time.

Q. Can I create multiple accounts using the same email id?

No, you cannot. It is against the policy of this grand app. Please read the Terms and Agreements before doing such...unwise stuff.
